About the Study

Welcome! We are an academic research team investigating the relationship between daily smartphone usage patterns and individual well-being. Your participation provides invaluable data for understanding modern digital psychology.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be 18 years of age or older.
  • Must be a regular smartphone user.
  • Fluent in reading English or Chinese.

What's Involved?

If you choose to participate, you will be asked to complete a brief online survey. It will take approximately 5 to 10 minutes. The questions will focus on your smartphone screen time, app usage habits, and general psychological well-being.

Privacy & Ethics

Your privacy is our utmost priority. All data collected will be strictly confidential and completely anonymized. The information will be used solely for academic research purposes and stored securely. This study complies with academic ethical standards.
