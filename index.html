About the Study

研究简介

Welcome to our online study. We are an academic research team from School of English Studies, Beijing International Studies University. This research investigates how native English speakers comprehend and process Chinese sentences. Your participation will support high-impact publications and advance linguistic research and international Chinese education.

3.1 Participation Content

3.1 参与内容

Study Structure (4 Experiments)

研究结构（4个实验）

  • Experiment 1 and 2: self-paced sentence reading only.
  • 实验1和实验2：仅包含自定步调句子阅读。
  • Experiment 3 and 4: sentence reading plus a working-memory test.
  • 实验3和实验4：包含句子阅读与工作记忆测试。
  • If you complete both Experiment 3 and 4, the memory test is required only once.
  • 若同时参与实验3和4，工作记忆测试只需完成一次。

Time and Task Composition

时间与任务构成

  1. Main reading task: about 50 minutes (Experiment 1/2), about 30 minutes (Experiment 3/4).
  2. 主阅读任务：实验1/2约50分钟，实验3/4约30分钟。
  3. Working-memory test: about 20 minutes (Experiment 3/4 only).
  4. 工作记忆测试：约20分钟（仅实验3/4）。

Actual duration may vary by reading speed and rest intervals.

实际用时会因个人阅读速度与休息安排略有差异。

3.2 Brief Outline of Tasks

3.2 任务简要说明

Step 3 includes two task types: sentence reading and working-memory test. All tasks are conducted online and require no software installation.

步骤3包含两类任务：句子阅读与工作记忆测试。所有任务均在线进行，无需安装软件。

a. Sentence Reading Task

a. 句子阅读任务

Read each sentence at your own pace and answer Yes/No comprehension questions.

您将按自定步调阅读句子，并回答是/否理解题。

b. Working Memory

b. 工作记忆测试

In Experiment 3/4, complete an Automated Operation Span Task: remember letter sequences while judging simple equations.

在实验3/4中，您需完成自动化操作广度任务：在记忆字母序列的同时判断简单算式正误。

⚠️ 3.6 Notes

⚠️ 3.6 注意事项

  • In the self-paced reading task, to ensure accurate timing, please do not pause while a sentence is on screen; instead, you may take breaks during the comprehension questions.
  • 请勿在句子呈现时暂停，建议在回答理解题时休息。
  • Please complete all questions carefully. If your accuracy is below 70%, your data will be invalid, and you will only receive $1 for each invalid experiment.
  • 请认真完成所有题目，如果正确率低于70%，将会视为无效数据，每个无效实验只能获取1美元报酬。
  • We sincerely thank all participants who successfully complete the experiment with exceptional performance, and we are pleased to award them an additional electronic Certificate of Honor in recognition of their outstanding contribution.
  • 我们由衷感谢所有以优异表现成功完成实验的参与者，并很高兴为他们颁发额外的电子荣誉证书，以表彰他们的杰出贡献。
  • The experiment will end on March 18. Please ensure you complete it before the deadline.
  • 实验将在3月18日结束。请确保在截止日期之前完成。
  • If the participants are minors, parents can help them understand the procedures beforehand, but please do not interfere or give instructions during the actual experiment.
  • 如果参与者是未成年人，家长可以帮助他们提前理解实验程序，但请不要在实际实验期间干预或给予指导。

Privacy & Confidentiality

隐私与保密

Your privacy is our utmost priority. All data collected will be strictly confidential, fully anonymised, and used solely for academic research purposes. Any directly identifying information (such as your name) will be removed and replaced with an anonymous code. The key linking this code to personal details will be stored securely and separately from research data. All personal information will be destroyed five years after project completion.

您的隐私是我们的首要任务。所有收集的数据将严格保密、完全匿名化，并仅用于学术研究目的。任何可直接识别身份的信息（如姓名）都将被删除并以匿名代码替代。用于关联匿名代码与个人信息的密钥将被安全、独立存储。所有个人信息将在项目完成五年后销毁。

Anonymised data may be made openly available via trusted repositories (e.g. Open Science Framework: https://osf.io/) to support future research. This study has been reviewed by the University Research Ethics Committee and has received a favourable ethical opinion. Participation is entirely voluntary, and you may withdraw at any time without giving a reason.

匿名化数据可能通过可信存储库（如开放科学框架：https://osf.io/）公开，以支持未来研究。本研究已通过大学研究伦理委员会审查并获得伦理批准。参与完全自愿，您可在任何时候无理由退出。
